Why This Website?

Many farmers in Tamil Nadu continue to struggle under heavy debts and uncertain crop outcomes. Their hard work often ends in loss due to unpredictable weather and market challenges. Uzhavan Connect was created to understand these real-life struggles directly from farmers and to collect authentic data that reflects their everyday reality.

Our Mission

To give farmers a voice that can reach policymakers and the public. Through this platform, we aim to document real challenges such as financial difficulties, drought impacts, and accessibility to government support.

These insights will serve as a foundation for creating better agricultural policies and building sustainable support systems for farmers.
